UNDP – The United Nations Development programme
We work in conjunction with the UNDP by co-funding projects and facilitating the transport of humanitarian aid, ensuring the safe arrival of goods donated by you. For more information on the UNDP please visit www.undp.org.


Governments of Kazakhstan, Russia and Ukraine
All of our projects are supported by the governments of Kazakhstan, Russia and the Ukraine.


Vincentian Order
We work with the Vincentian Order to provide support for homeless people in Kiev. Visit www.vincentians.ie for more information.


Fathers House International Kiev Ukraine
Assisted by the Greater Chernobyl Cause, Father’s House International provides education, a home and long term rehabilitation for some of the thousands of street children in the Ukrainian capital of Kiev.
